## About the Client

<Gallery layout={[{ width: 70 }, { imageKey: "clients/psyches_of_color", width: 25 }]}>

Psyches of Color uses strengths-based and culturally relevant approaches to provide mental health support to at-promise Black and Latinx youth and aims to decrease the stigma of mental health and promote radical healing.

</Gallery>

## Executive Summary

We are building a mobile application that will enable users to complete mental health activities, journals, and check-ins, as well as view accessible support resources.

The project is currently under development and scheduled to finish around June!

For more information on the technical aspects of the project, please visit [https://github.com/TritonSE/Psyches-Of-Color-App](https://github.com/TritonSE/Psyches-Of-Color-App).
